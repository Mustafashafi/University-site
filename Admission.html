Undergraduate Admissions

Applying to ScholarlyU is an exciting step towards joining a vibrant community of scholars and innovators. We seek students who are not only academically strong but also demonstrate a passion for learning and a commitment to making a difference. Our holistic review process considers all aspects of your application, including academic achievements, personal essays, extracurricular activities, and letters of recommendation.

Financial Aid

We believe that financial constraints should not prevent talented students from pursuing their dreams. ScholarlyU offers a range of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study programs. We encourage all applicants to explore these opportunities and apply for aid as early as possible.
